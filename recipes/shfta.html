Shfta Recipe

Description

This is Shfta, a Kurdish meat patties that is popular all throughout Kurdistan and in Kurdish homes around the world.

Ingerdients

  • 1 KG Ground meat (beef preferred)
  • 2 Medium onions
  • 1 small bunch Celery green leaves - chopped or grated
  • 1 - 2 tablespoons Tomato paste
  • 200g Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a medium bowl and mix together.

  2. Roll the mixture into 1 inch balls and press flat with a fork.

  3. Heat the pan and pour in the oil.

  4. When the oil is hot, fry the patties on both sides until golden.
